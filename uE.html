Instructions

Welcome, young horse enthusiast!


To start, buy horses at the top of gameplay.

To evolve your horses into a greater form, drag two of the same breed together. See how many new horse breeds you can find!

You will win the game when the final horse has been reached. Good luck!


The controls for the background music are at the bottom left of your screen.
Note: In some browsers you will have to manually start the background music.



This game works in Chrome, Edge, Opera, and Firefox. This game does not work in Internet Explorer.
